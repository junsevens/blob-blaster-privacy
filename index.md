# Blob Blaster Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

## Information We Collect
Blob Blaster does not collect any personal information. The game stores only your best score locally on your device.

## Data Storage
All game data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

## Third Party Services
This app does not use any third party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy
This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
